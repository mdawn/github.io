Software User Guide

Role: Sole Author

Industry: Software / Educational

Tools: Google docs, Google drive, Screenshots, Pages, Preview, Laulima

Description: This is a sampling of additional instructional user guides I created to supplement commercial software. The audience for these documents were a cohort of ~30 non-college-educated minority indigenous adults from disadvantaged backgrounds who had limited access to computers and, in some cases, limited access to electricity. I'd received complaints that the user guide Bedford provided were not clear enough. After some review, I agreed that Bedford's commercially-developed instructions were lacking. I quickly made these two Five-Step Quick-Start guides with screenshots in response to the complaints. Complaints decreased immediately. The following cohorts of adults (who used the guide from the beginning) had no complaints.

Duration: 2011-2013 (~2 years)

https:/docs.google.com/document/d/1Srb0Ge7Eh9stg9u0wCGKgHXQQ5PTghN4AtNYBrurO7c/edit?usp=sharing
